# Glossary

- **OBS (Öğrenci Bilgi Sistemi):** Student Information System.
- **Advisor (Akademik Danışman):** Professor responsible for reviewing and approving student course registrations.
- **Double Major (Çift Anadal / ÇAP):** Program allowing students to pursue two distinct undergraduate degrees simultaneously.
- **Substitution / Exemption (İntibak / Muafiyet):** The process of mapping a new or different course to cover a failed or removed course.
- **Midterm (Vize):** The intermediate exam held mid-semester.
- **Final:** The final exam held at the end of the semester.
- **GPA / CGPA (GNO):** Grade Point Average / Cumulative Grade Point Average.
- **ECTS (AKTS):** European Credit Transfer and Accumulation System.
